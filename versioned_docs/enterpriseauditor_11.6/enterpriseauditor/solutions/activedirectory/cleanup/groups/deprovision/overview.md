# 1.Deprovision Job Group

The 1. Deprovision Groups Job Group provides a simple, automated workflow to deprovision stale groups. The action tasks in this job group provide an automated workflow.

![1.Deprovision Job Group in the Jobs Tree](/img/versioned_docs/enterpriseauditor_11.6/enterpriseauditor/solutions/activedirectory/cleanup/groups/deprovision/groupsdeprovisionjobtree.png)

The jobs in the 1. Deprovision Groups Job Group are:

- [AD\_DeprovisionGroups Job](/versioned_docs/enterpriseauditor_11.6/enterpriseauditor/solutions/activedirectory/cleanup/groups/deprovision/ad_deprovisiongroups.md) – This job provides an automated workflow to deprovision stale groups
- [AD\_DeprovisionGroups\_Status Job](/versioned_docs/enterpriseauditor_11.6/enterpriseauditor/solutions/activedirectory/cleanup/groups/deprovision/ad_deprovisiongroups_status.md) – This job tracks and reports on the progress of all actions taken by the included Deprovisioning workflow
