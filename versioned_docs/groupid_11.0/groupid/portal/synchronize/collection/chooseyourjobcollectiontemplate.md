# Choose your Job Template

For creating a job, GroupID Synchronize enables you to create a new job collection based on your setting and criteria. In addition to that, it also gives you multiple templates to directly create a job collection from.

__Follow these steps:__

1. On GroupID Portal, select __Synchronize__ on left pane.
2. On the Synchronize portal, click __Create New__ and then click __Job Collection.__
3. On the __Choose Your Job Template__ page, enter job collection details and select whether to use a job group template or create the job group from
   scratch.
4. Click __Next Step__.
