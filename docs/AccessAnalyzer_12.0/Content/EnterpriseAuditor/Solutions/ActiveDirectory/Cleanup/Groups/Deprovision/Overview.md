# 1.Deprovision Job Group

The 1. Deprovision Groups Job Group provides a simple, automated workflow to deprovision stale groups. The action tasks in this job group provide an automated workflow.

![1.Deprovision Job Group in the Jobs Tree](../../../../../../../../../static/images/AccessAnalyzer_12.0/Content/Resources/Images/EnterpriseAuditor/Solutions/ActiveDirectory/Cleanup/GroupsDeprovisionJobTree.png "1.Deprovision Job Group in the Jobs Tree")

The jobs in the 1. Deprovision Groups Job Group are:

* [AD\_DeprovisionGroups Job](AD_DeprovisionGroups "AD_DeprovisionGroups Job") – This job provides an automated workflow to deprovision stale groups
* [AD\_DeprovisionGroups\_Status Job](AD_DeprovisionGroups_Status "AD_DeprovisionGroups_Status Job") – This job tracks and reports on the progress of all actions taken by the included Deprovisioning workflow
