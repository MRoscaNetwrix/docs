# 0.Collection Job Group

The jobs within this group collect NFS and Samba configuration information which will be further analyzed to identify and categorize risk within audited Unix and Linux environments.

![0.Collection Job Group in the Jobs Tree](../../../../../../../../static/images/AccessAnalyzer_12.0/Content/Resources/Images/EnterpriseAuditor/Solutions/Unix/Sharing/CollectionJobsTree.png "0.Collection Job Group in the Jobs Tree")

The jobs in the 0.Collection job group are:

* [UX\_NFSConfiguration Job](UX_NFSConfiguration "UX_NFSConfiguration Job") – Collects NFS configuration information which will be further analyzed to identify and categorize risk within audited Unix and Linux environments
* [UX\_NFSConfiguration Job](UX_NFSConfiguration "UX_NFSConfiguration Job") – Collects Samba configuration information which will be further analyzed to identify and categorize risk within audited Unix and Linux environments
