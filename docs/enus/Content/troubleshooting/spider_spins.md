# Spider Spins and Does Not Finish

If you kick off a spider and the gears on the page spin for more than an hour, this is likely an issue with the **IP Restriction**. Contact Netwrix support at [https://www.netwrix.com/support.html](https://www.netwrix.com/support.html "Open the Netwrix Support site")  for assistance in configuring your account.
