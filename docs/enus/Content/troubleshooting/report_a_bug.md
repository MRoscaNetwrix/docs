# Contact Support

If you encounter any problems using Platform Governance for NetSuite or you have suggested improvements, we would love to hear from you! Your feedback is incredibly valuable to us and the continued success of our products. There are multiple options to provide feedback:

* [Contact Support](#Contact "Jump to Contact Support section")
* [Comment on a User Guide Topic](#Comment "Jump to Comment on a User Guide Topic section")

## Contact Support

You can contact Support with any feedback or issues. There are links within Platform Governance for NetSuite to make it easy to access the Netwrix support site at [https://www.netwrix.com/support.html](https://www.netwrix.com/support.html "Open the Netwrix Support site")

* Click **Submit a Support Case** from the [Strongpoint Overview Dashboard](../navigating_strongpoint "Open Navigating Strongpoint topic to see information on the Overview Dashboard") in your Production or Sandbox accounts.
* Open **Strongpoint** > **Strongpoint Support** > **Contact Support** to open the Netwrix Support site.

![](../../../../static/images/enus/Content/Resources/Images/report_bug.png "Contact Strongpoint Support")

## Comment on a User Guide Topic

There is a comment button at the end of each topic, enabling you to easily send feedback to Support on the topic.
