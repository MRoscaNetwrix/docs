# Scheduling Commands

This section covers the cmdlets that perform scheduling-related operations.

- [Get-Schedule](/docs/groupid/groupid/managementshell/scheduling/getschedule.md) – retrieves scheduled jobs.
- [Get-TargetSchedules](/docs/groupid/groupid/managementshell/scheduling/gettargetschedule.md) – retrieves the scheduled jobs operating on a group or OU.
- [ Invoke-Schedule](/docs/groupid/groupid/managementshell/scheduling/invokeschedule.md) – executes a scheduled job.
- [New-Schedule](/docs/groupid/groupid/managementshell/scheduling/newschedule.md) – creates a new schedule.
- [Remove-Schedule](/docs/groupid/groupid/managementshell/scheduling/removeschedule.md) – removes a schedule from an identity store.
- [Set-Schedule](/docs/groupid/groupid/managementshell/scheduling/setschedule.md) – modifies a schedule.
- [Stop-Schedule](/docs/groupid/groupid/managementshell/scheduling/stopschedule.md) – stops a running schedule.
