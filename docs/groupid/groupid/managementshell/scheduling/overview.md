# Scheduling Commands

This section covers the cmdlets that perform scheduling-related operations.

- [Get-Schedule](getschedule.md) – retrieves scheduled jobs.
- [Get-TargetSchedules](gettargetschedule.md) – retrieves the scheduled jobs operating on a group or OU.
- [ Invoke-Schedule](invokeschedule.md) – executes a scheduled job.
- [New-Schedule](newschedule.md) – creates a new schedule.
- [Remove-Schedule](removeschedule.md) – removes a schedule from an identity store.
- [Set-Schedule](setschedule.md) – modifies a schedule.
- [Stop-Schedule](stopschedule.md) – stops a running schedule.
