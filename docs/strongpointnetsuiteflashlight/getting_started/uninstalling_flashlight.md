# Uninstalling Flashlight

Here is how to uninstall the Flashlight bundle from your account:

1. From the NetSuite main menu, hover over __Customization__ > __SuiteBundler__ > __Search & Install__ > __List__
2. Locate the Flashlight bundle in installed bundle list.
3. Hover over the icon in the __Action__ column and click __Uninstall__

   ![uninstall_flashlight_800x226](/img/product_docs/strongpointnetsuiteflashlight/getting_started/uninstall_flashlight_800x226.webp)
