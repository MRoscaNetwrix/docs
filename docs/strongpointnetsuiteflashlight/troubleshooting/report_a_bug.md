# Report a Bug

If you encounter any problems using Flashlight or you have suggested improvements, we would love to hear from you! Your feedback is incredibly valuable to us and the continued success of Flashlight. You can access the Netwrix Support site at [https://www.netwrix.com/support.html](https://www.netwrix.com/support.html) or access it from within Flashlight:

1. Open __Flashlight__ > __Support__ > __Report a Bug__
2. Click on __Submit a Ticket__ and follow the prompts.
