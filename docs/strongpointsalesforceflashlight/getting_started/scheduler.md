# Running the Scheduler

Strongpoint's scheduler creates automated scans and documents the Field Usage and date last used (DLU).

To use the scheduler tool:

1. Open __Flashlight__ > __Support__ > __Scheduler__
![scheduler](/static/img/product_docs/strongpointforsalesforce/clean_up/scheduler.png)2. Toggle __Enabled__/__Disabled__by the category. Your selections are automatically saved.
3. Select the __Frequency__ , __Day__ and __Time__. Your selections are automatically saved.
4. Click any menu item to close the __Scheduler__.
