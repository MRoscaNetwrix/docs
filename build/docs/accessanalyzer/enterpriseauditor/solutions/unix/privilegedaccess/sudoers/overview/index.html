Sudoers Job Group

The Sudoers job group provides visibility into all rights granted via sudoers within audited Unix and Linux environments.

The jobs in the Sudoers job group are:

  • 0.Collection Job Group – This group collects details on all rights granted via sudoers within audited Unix and Linux environments
  • UX_Sudoers Job – This job details all rights granted via sudoers in the audited environment
