Global Event Ranges​

Event IDsDescription
100-199Events related to policy processing, and specifically Group Policy processing.
200-299General events, such as errors that don't belong to any other category.
300-599Product-specific events, such as errors that don't belong to any other category.
600-9999Events related to operational activities, such as allowing, blocking or elevating a process
